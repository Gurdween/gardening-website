Plant Guide

Explore our comprehensive plant categories and discover the perfect additions to your garden

Flowering Plants

Beautiful blooms that add color and fragrance to your garden throughout the seasons

• Water regularly but avoid overwatering

• Provide 6-8 hours of sunlight daily

• Deadhead spent blooms for continued flowering

Herbs & Vegetables

Fresh, homegrown produce and aromatic herbs for your kitchen and table

• Rich, well-draining soil is essential

• Regular harvesting encourages growth

• Companion planting improves yields

Trees & Shrubs

Structural plants that provide shade, privacy, and year-round garden interest

• Plant in fall or early spring

• Deep watering less frequently

• Prune during dormant season

Indoor Plants

Bring nature indoors with low-maintenance houseplants that purify air

• Bright, indirect light preferred

• Allow soil to dry between waterings

• Dust leaves regularly for health
